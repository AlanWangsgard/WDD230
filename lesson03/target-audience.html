Target Audience

The target audience is anyone who needs to know what the weather will be like. They could need to know what It will be like for recreational purposes, or professional.

Persona 1

Steve: Steve is an accountant, and has 3 children. He enjoys spending time outdoors with his children in the outdoors. He tries to spend as much time as he can in the outdoors, living life and seeing all that the earth has to offer.

Persona 2

Chad: Chad owns a small landscaping buisiness and spends a vast majority of his time in the outdoors. He has many clients that have properties that he has to maintain, or update if the owner wishes.

Scenerio 1

Kevin is trying to plan a hiking trip for this weekend and is trying to figure out how warm he should dress. He also needs to know if he should bring rain gear. He checks the sitte and sees that it will be sunny, so he can dress accordingly for his hike.

Scenerio 2

Stacy is planning a birthday party for her daughter next week. She is going to have a bounce house and a opiniata. She wants to make sure that it will not rain and no one will get wet. She checks the site and sees that it will rain the day she had planned to have the party, so she reschedules it.

Scenerio 3

Seth and his friends are going on a ski tip and want to make sure that it will be fun, and there will be enough snow to have a great time. They discover that it will snow the day before they go skiing, so they will have a great time.

Scenerio 4

Corinne is going to drive to her brothers house, but the roads have been icy. She looks at the site and sees that It will be sunny for the next few days. She decides to go and visit her brother after the sunny days, so more of the ice melts.

Scenerio 5

Luke is going mountain biking with his father this weekend and wants to make sure the trails will be clear. he goes on the site and sees that it will be cloudy, but not rain, so it will be a great day for biking.
